Hello.

I am an accomplished photographer, videographer, graphic designer, and web designer, with a passion for creating stunning visuals that captivate and inspire. Born and raised in the vibrant country of Iran, I have always been drawn to the power of visual communication, and it was this fascination that led me to pursue a career in media design. After completing my Bachelor's degree in English Translation at Isfahan University in Iran, I found myself increasingly drawn to the world of design. It all began when I volunteered to design the faculty's foreign language magazine as a side project. This experience ignited a spark within me, and I soon realized that this was my calling. Each year, as I continued to refine my skills and expand my knowledge, my passion for design grew stronger. In pursuit of my dreams, I decided to move to Ottawa, Canada, to further my education and develop my skills in this field. It was a bold move, but one that has paid off in spades. Today, I am proud to be a highly skilled and versatile designer, with a wealth of experience across multiple mediums and platforms. Whether I am behind the camera, designing a website, or creating a graphic masterpiece, my goal is always the same: to create work that is visually stunning, emotionally powerful, and unforgettable. And with each new project, I continue to push the boundaries of what is possible, always striving for excellence and innovation. My journey in media design has been an exciting and fulfilling one, and I am excited to see where it will take me next.
